About DV Aviation Private Jet Charter

DV Aviation is a direct-operator private jet charter company headquartered in Midland, Texas, serving the Permian Basin oil and gas industry since establishment. Unlike charter brokers who markup third-party aircraft, DV Aviation owns and operates its own fleet of aircraft, ensuring direct accountability, transparent pricing with no broker fees, and guaranteed aircraft availability.

Fleet Information

The Bombardier Learjet 45XR is a light jet capable of cruising at 535 miles per hour, seating 8 passengers comfortably, with a maximum range of 2,049 nautical miles. It completes the Midland to Houston route in approximately 60 minutes. The aircraft features corporate Wi-Fi connectivity for airborne productivity, making it ideal for executives who need to work during transit.

The Beechcraft King Air B200 is a twin-turboprop aircraft seating up to 10 passengers, capable of landing on runways as short as 2,500 feet. This short-field capability makes it ideal for accessing remote oil field locations, private airstrips, and smaller regional airports that commercial jets and larger aircraft cannot reach. The King Air B200 is the preferred choice for crew rotations, multi-site field inspections, and AOG (Aircraft on Ground) emergency parts delivery throughout the Permian Basin region.

Service Area and Destinations

DV Aviation primarily serves the Permian Basin oil and gas region including Midland, Odessa, Andrews, Pecos, Monahans, and surrounding areas. Regular flight destinations include Houston (William P. Hobby Airport and George Bush Intercontinental Airport), Dallas (Dallas Love Field and Dallas/Fort Worth International Airport), Austin-Bergstrom International Airport, San Antonio International Airport, and destinations throughout Texas and the Gulf Coast region. The company operates from Midland International Air and Space Port (MAF).

Services Offered

DV Aviation provides comprehensive aviation services including on-demand private jet charter, scheduled crew rotation flights, emergency AOG parts delivery with less than 4-hour response time guarantee, executive air travel, aircraft management, aircraft maintenance with OEM-trained technicians, aircraft acquisition consulting, and aircraft sales. All operations are conducted under FAA Part 135 regulations with ARGUS Gold safety certification.
